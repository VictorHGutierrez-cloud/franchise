    - **üéØ ***PPL Management Structure - Fundamental Metrics*
        - **1. PPL Stage (Pipeline Stage)**
            
            *Defines the negotiation phase based on the prospect's alignment and engagement:*
            ‚Ä¢ **Demo:** Initial meeting/demonstration scheduled.
            ‚Ä¢ **Product Alignment**: After the first call. Opportunity in the discovery and in-depth process mapping phase.
            ‚Ä¢ **Pricing and Packaging** : Value and processes aligned. In the phase of drafting the package, budget, and module definition.
            ‚Ä¢ **Contracting**: Verbal commitment established. In the contract negotiation and procurement process phase.
            ‚Ä¢ **Won**: Contract signed. Opportunity closed as won.
            ‚Ä¢ **Lost**: Opportunity closed as lost.
            
        - **2. Next Steps**
            
            *Indicates the crucial next action required to advance the opportunity:*
            ‚Ä¢ **[Meeting Date]** + **[Topic/Action]**: Meeting already scheduled (Ex: Dec 5 - Financial Alignment Meeting).
            ‚Ä¢ **TBD** + **[Action to Book Meeting]**: No meeting scheduled. An action must be taken to ensure the next touchpoint (Ex: TBD - Send proposal with 3 dates and follow up).
            
        - **Forecast**
            
            *Defines the level of certainty and urgency of the opportunity, indicating the probability of closing:*
            ‚Ä¢ **Pipeline** (Cold):
                ‚ó¶ Cold, outside BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timing) criteria.
                ‚ó¶ Only 1 or 2 BANT dimensions aligned.
                ‚ó¶ **Probability: 55% or less**.
            ‚Ä¢ **Upside** (Hot):
                ‚ó¶ Hot, outside BANT criteria (but with high potential).
                ‚ó¶ 3 or 4 BANT dimensions aligned.
                ‚ó¶ **Probability: 70%+**.
            ‚Ä¢ **Commit** (Verbally Committed):
                ‚ó¶ Verbal commitment to close.
                ‚ó¶ Minimal risk, awaiting formalization.
                ‚ó¶ **Probability: 90%+**.
            ‚Ä¢ **Lost**: Opportunity closed as lost.
