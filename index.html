Strategic Analysis for Investors

The Investment Valley (J-Curve)

Year 1 is focused on building the portfolio (Building the Book). We end the year with accumulated net investment of ~$178k (including entry fee). This is normal and expected for a recurring revenue model.

The Magic: Year 2 (Asset Value)

On Day 1 of Month 13, you start Year 2 already billing $8,000/month without making any new sales. With the team maintaining 5 sales/month, monthly break-even happens around Month 18-20.

Cash Flow Optimization

Important Note: This table simulates the "Worst Case Scenario" (all customers paying monthly). If we convert 50% of customers to Annual Contracts (Advance Payment), cash flow improves drastically, accelerating break-even by 4 to 6 months.
