### üéØ PPL Management Structure - Fundamental Metrics

#### 1. PPL Stage (Pipeline Stage)

*Defines the negotiation phase based on the prospect's alignment and engagement:*

- **Demo:** Initial meeting/demonstration scheduled.
- **Product Alignment:** After the first call. Opportunity in the discovery and in-depth process mapping phase.
- **Pricing and Packaging:** Value and processes aligned. In the phase of drafting the package, budget, and module definition.
- **Contracting:** Verbal commitment established. In the contract negotiation and procurement process phase.
- **Won:** Contract signed. Opportunity closed as won.
- **Lost:** Opportunity closed as lost.

#### 2. Next Steps

*Indicates the crucial next action required to advance the opportunity:*

- **[Meeting Date]** + **[Topic/Action]:** Meeting already scheduled (Ex: Dec 5 - Financial Alignment Meeting).
- **TBD** + **[Action to Book Meeting]:** No meeting scheduled. An action must be taken to ensure the next touchpoint (Ex: TBD - Send proposal with 3 dates and follow up).

#### 3. Forecast

*Defines the level of certainty and urgency of the opportunity, indicating the probability of closing:*

- **Pipeline** (Cold):
  - Cold, outside BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timing) criteria.
  - Only 1 or 2 BANT dimensions aligned.
  - **Probability: 55% or less**.
- **Upside** (Hot):
  - Hot, outside BANT criteria (but with high potential).
  - 3 or 4 BANT dimensions aligned.
  - **Probability: 70%+**.
- **Commit** (Verbally Committed):
  - Verbal commitment to close.
  - Minimal risk, awaiting formalization.
  - **Probability: 90%+**.
- **Lost:** Opportunity closed as lost.
